About RPI CSE Community

A student-led tech community fostering innovation, collaboration, and skill development across all CSE disciplines at Rajshahi Polytechnic Institute.

The RPI CSE Community was established to bridge the gap between academic learning and real-world technology. We bring together passionate students, faculty advisors, and industry mentors to create a thriving ecosystem of learning and innovation.

Through our clubs, events, workshops, and competitions, members gain hands-on experience in cutting-edge technologies — from artificial intelligence to embedded systems and competitive programming.
